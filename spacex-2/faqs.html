What is SpaceX Dragon?

The SpaceX Dragon spacecraft is a family of spacecraft developed and operated by SpaceX. It was designed to carry cargo and crew to the International Space Station (ISS). How many Dragon missions have there been?

As of 2024, SpaceX has completed over 30 Dragon missions, including resupply missions to the ISS and crewed flights. The Dragon spacecraft has been an essential part of SpaceX's partnership with NASA and other space agencies.

What are the benefits of using Dragon for space missions?

The SpaceX Dragon spacecraft is fully reusable, which makes it more cost-effective for space missions. It also features state-of-the-art technology, such as advanced life-support systems for crewed missions and an upgraded cargo capacity for resupply missions.
