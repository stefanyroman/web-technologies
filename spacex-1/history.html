SpaceX Dragon Development

The SpaceX Dragon spacecraft was developed by SpaceX with the goal of revolutionizing space transportation. First launched in 2010, Dragon became the first privately developed spacecraft to deliver cargo to the International Space Station (ISS). Over the years, it has evolved, and with the Dragon 2 model, it now carries both cargo and crew.
